Our Mission

Boardwalk Clay Nonprofit exists to rebuild what has been forgotten — both in the community and within the youth who serve it. We focus on:

  • Transforming neglected lots, unsafe trees, and trash-filled areas
  • Training youth in real-world skills like tree climbing, safety, and environmental care
  • Providing mentorship that builds confidence, discipline, and leadership
  • Creating a cycle of service where youth give back to the same communities they grew up in

The Community Service page dives deeper into the work we do, the neighborhoods we serve, and the impact we create every week.

Youth Training

Our youth training program is built around hands-on experience. Young people don’t just learn — they participate, lead, and transform their environment.

  • Tree climbing and safety fundamentals
  • Tool handling, teamwork, and communication
  • Environmental awareness and stewardship
  • Real service projects that build pride and purpose

The Youth Training page explains how the program works, what youth learn, and how families can get involved.

Community Service

We go where help is needed most — abandoned lots, overgrown areas, unsafe trees, and forgotten corners of the community. Our teams restore, rebuild, and bring life back to these spaces.

  • Neighborhood cleanup days
  • Tree trimming and hazard removal
  • Light building and repair projects
  • Youth-led service missions guided by mentors

The Community Service page shows the full scope of our work, upcoming service opportunities, and how you can join us.

Sponsorships

Sponsorships are the backbone of our mission. They directly support the people doing the work — the youth learning the skills and the mentors guiding them.

  • Sponsor a Youth — fund training, gear, safety equipment, and transportation
  • Sponsor a Mentor — support the leaders who teach, protect, and guide youth
  • Project Sponsorship — fund specific cleanup or restoration missions

The Sponsorships page breaks down each option and shows how your support creates real change.
