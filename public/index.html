Boardwalk Clay Organization

A Non-Profit for Youth, Community & Restoration

We empower youth through real arborist training, hands-on service, leadership development, and community restoration. Our mission is to rebuild forgotten spaces, uplift neighborhoods, and guide young people into purpose, discipline, and confidence. Every cleanup, every climb, every project is a step toward a stronger community — and a stronger generation.

🌱 Our Mission

We transform neglected spaces, train young leaders, and create opportunities for real change. Our mission is rooted in growth — of youth, neighborhoods, and community strength.

🌿 Youth Training

Youth learn real arborist skills, teamwork, safety, environmental care, and leadership — all through hands-on service.

🌳 Community Service

We restore abandoned lots, remove hazards, clean neighborhoods, and rebuild forgotten areas.

🤝 Sponsorships

Sponsorships fund youth training, gear, safety equipment, transportation, and community restoration projects.

📅 Events

Cleanup days, youth workshops, tree work events, and community gatherings — all open to volunteers.

💚 Donate

Every donation fuels our mission — from tools to training to mentor support.
