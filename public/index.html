Empowering Youth. Restoring Communities.

Boardwalk Clay Nonprofit transforms neglected spaces, trains youth in real-world skills, and builds stronger communities through mentorship, service, and hands-on environmental care.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple but powerful: uplift the community by training youth, restoring neglected areas, and providing hands-on mentorship in tree work, cleanup, building, and environmental care.

We believe every young person deserves the chance to learn real skills, build confidence, and contribute to something bigger than themselves.

Youth Training

Our youth training program teaches tree climbing, safety, teamwork, environmental awareness, and leadership through real community projects.

Youth learn from experienced mentors and gain skills that last a lifetime.

Community Service

We take on neglected areas—overgrown lots, unsafe trees, trash-filled corners—and restore them with the help of youth, mentors, and volunteers.

Cleanup days, tree work, restoration projects, and community events happen year-round.

Sponsorships

Sponsorships directly support the people doing the work—youth trainees and the mentors who guide them.

You can sponsor a mentor, sponsor a youth, or support a specific project.

Events

Join us for cleanup days, tree work volunteer events, youth workshops, and community restoration projects.
