Our Mission

Boardwalk Clay Organization exists to rebuild what has been forgotten — both in our neighborhoods and in the youth who serve them. We focus on transforming neglected spaces, providing hands-on training, and creating opportunities for young people to grow into leaders.

When you visit the Community Service page, you’ll see the real work we do: clearing overgrown lots, removing unsafe trees, restoring damaged areas, and bringing life back to forgotten spaces.

Youth Training

Our youth training program gives young people real-world experience in tree climbing, safety, teamwork, environmental care, and leadership. They don’t just learn — they participate in real service projects that build confidence, discipline, and purpose.

The Youth Training page explains how the program works, what youth learn, and how families can get involved.

Community Service

We go where help is needed most — abandoned lots, overgrown areas, unsafe trees, and forgotten corners of the community. Our teams restore, rebuild, and bring life back to these spaces through consistent, hands-on work.

On the Community Service page, you’ll see upcoming service opportunities, past projects, and ways you can join us in the field.

Sponsorships

Sponsorships are the backbone of our mission. They directly support the youth learning the skills and the mentors guiding them. Your sponsorship helps fund training, gear, safety equipment, transportation, and community restoration projects.

The Sponsorships page breaks down each option and shows how your support creates real change.

Events

Our events bring the community together — volunteers, youth, mentors, and supporters all working side-by-side. From cleanup days to youth workshops, every event is an opportunity to make a difference.

The Events page lists upcoming opportunities and lets you sign up instantly.

Donate

Every donation fuels our mission — from gear and tools to youth training and mentor support. Your contribution helps us expand our reach and restore more communities.

The Donate page explains how your support is used and how you can contribute.

Contact Us

Whether you want to volunteer, partner with us, sponsor a youth, or simply learn more — we’re here to talk. The Contact page gives you direct access to our email, phone number, and social platforms.
