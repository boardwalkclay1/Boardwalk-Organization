Our Mentors

What Mentors Do

Mentors are more than instructors—they are protectors, guides, and examples.

  • Teach safe tree climbing and tool handling
  • Lead youth in real service projects
  • Model responsibility, discipline, and care
  • Help youth see their value and potential
