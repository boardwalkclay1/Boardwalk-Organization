🧹 Neighborhood Cleanup Day

Date: Every 2nd Saturday

Location: Rotating neighborhoods

Join us as we clear trash, trim overgrowth, restore neglected areas, and bring life back to forgotten spaces. These cleanup days are youth‑led with mentor support, building leadership and teamwork.

🌳 Tree Work Volunteer Day

Date: Monthly

Location: Announced weekly

Assist mentors and youth with safe tree trimming, hazard removal, rope work, and environmental care. No experience required — just willingness to learn and help.

🌱 Youth Training Workshop

Date: Twice per month

Location: Boardwalk Clay Training Grounds

Hands‑on training in tree climbing, safety, teamwork, leadership, and environmental stewardship. Youth learn real skills from real mentors in the field.
