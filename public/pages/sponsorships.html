Sponsorships

Why Sponsorship Matters

Sponsorships are the engine behind Boardwalk Clay’s work. They support youth training, mentor time, safety gear, transportation, and community projects.

When you sponsor, you’re not just giving money — you’re giving skills, safety, and opportunity.

What Sponsorship Supports

Youth Sponsorship

Covers helmets, gloves, ropes, harnesses, training days, transportation, and meals on long service days.

Mentor Sponsorship

Covers mentor time, safety oversight, training materials, certifications, and fuel/equipment maintenance.

Community Project Sponsorship

Covers cleanup supplies, dump fees, tools, hazard removal, and restoration materials.

Sponsorship Levels

Seed Sponsor — $25

Helps provide basic youth gear and safety items.

Sapling Sponsor — $50

Supports one youth training day with mentors.

Tree Sponsor — $100

Supports a full youth training week, including gear and transportation.

Forest Sponsor — $250

Supports a community cleanup or restoration project.

Legacy Sponsor — $500+

Supports certifications, advanced gear, and long‑term youth development.

What Sponsors Receive
  • Recognition on the website (optional)
  • A sponsor badge or digital certificate
  • Updates on youth progress and projects
  • Invitations to community events
  • A tax‑deductible receipt
How to Sponsor

You can sponsor in two ways:

  1. Choose a sponsorship level and give through our Donate page.
  2. Contact us directly to set up a custom sponsorship.
