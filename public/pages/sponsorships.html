💚 Why Sponsorship Matters

🌲 Sponsor a Mentor

Mentors are the backbone of our organization. They climb, teach, protect, and guide youth through real-world service projects — from tree work to community restoration.

Your sponsorship helps provide:

  • Professional safety gear and climbing equipment
  • Transportation to community service sites
  • Time spent training and supervising youth
  • Ongoing certifications and skill development

When you sponsor a mentor, you’re investing in leadership, safety, and the future of every youth they train.

🌱 Sponsor a Youth

Many young people want to learn, serve, and grow — but cost can be a barrier. Your sponsorship removes that barrier and opens the door to real opportunity.

Your support can provide:

  • Training program enrollment
  • Helmets, harnesses, gloves, and safety gear
  • Ropes, tools, and climbing equipment
  • Transportation and meals on service days

Sponsoring a youth means giving them confidence, purpose, and a path to leadership.

🌿 Sponsorship Levels

  • Seed Level – Helps with basic gear and supplies.
  • Sapling Level – Supports a youth or mentor for a specific project.
  • Canopy Level – Funds a full training cycle or multiple service days.
  • Legacy Level – Provides ongoing support across seasons and programs.

Every level makes a real impact — choose the one that fits your heart and your mission.

💚 Why Sponsorship Matters

Sponsorship is more than financial support — it’s partnership. You become part of every cleanup, every restored space, every youth trained, and every life changed.

You help:

  • Turn neglected spaces into safe, beautiful areas
  • Equip youth with real skills and confidence
  • Support mentors who model leadership and care
  • Build a culture of service, responsibility, and community pride
