Sponsorships

Sponsor a Mentor

Mentors are the backbone of our work. They climb, teach, protect, and guide youth in real‑world environments.

Your sponsorship helps cover:

  • Time spent training and supervising youth
  • Safety gear and climbing equipment
  • Transportation to service sites
  • Ongoing skill development and certifications
Sponsor a Youth

Many young people want to learn, serve, and grow—but cost can be a barrier.

Your sponsorship can:

  • Cover training program fees
  • Provide helmets, harnesses, and safety gear
  • Fund gloves, ropes, and tools
  • Support transportation and meals on service days
Sponsorship Levels
  • Seed Level – Help with basic gear and supplies.
  • Sapling Level – Support a youth or mentor for a specific project.
  • Canopy Level – Fund a full training cycle or multiple service days.
  • Legacy Level – Ongoing support across seasons and programs.
Why Sponsorship Matters

Sponsorship is more than money—it’s partnership.

You’re helping:

  • Turn neglected spaces into safe, beautiful areas
  • Equip youth with real skills and confidence
  • Support mentors who model leadership and care
  • Build a culture of service and responsibility
