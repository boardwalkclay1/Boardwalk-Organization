Youth Training Programs

🌱 Program Overview

🌱 Program Overview

Our youth training program is built around real work, real responsibility, and real mentorship. We don’t teach from a classroom — we teach from the field, where youth learn hands-on skills that build confidence, discipline, and purpose.

Every youth in our program learns:

  • Tree climbing fundamentals and professional safety practices
  • Tool handling, rope management, and situational awareness
  • Environmental stewardship and respect for nature
  • Teamwork, leadership, communication, and accountability
🌿 Youth Mentorship Pathway

🌿 Youth Mentorship Pathway

Our youth follow the same structured mentorship path described on the Mentors page, adapted for their age and readiness.

Phase 1 — Orientation & Safety

Introduction to PPE, knots, rope handling, ground safety, and communication.

Phase 2 — Ground Operations

Raking, hauling, rope management, spotting, and working as a team.

Phase 3 — Beginner Climbing

Basic ascent, controlled descent, and safe movement in the canopy under full supervision.

Phase 4 — Intermediate Skills

Rigging basics, hazard awareness, non-powered saw safety, and environmental care.

Phase 5 — Leadership & Service

Youth help lead small teams during community projects and service days.

Phase 6 — Certification & Career Path

For youth who want to go further, we support certification prep and job pathways.

🌳 Professional Tree Climbing & Safety

🌳 Professional Tree Climbing & Safety

Youth learn professional tree climbing from experienced mentors who work in the field every day. This is not recreational climbing — this is real arborist training, taught with industry‑standard gear and safety protocols.

Youth learn how to:

  • Use harnesses, saddles, helmets, and ropes correctly
  • Perform safe ascents and controlled descents
  • Inspect trees for health, hazards, and structural weaknesses
  • Move confidently and safely in elevated environments
  • Respect the environment while working in it
📘 Business & Job Skills Training

📘 Business & Job Skills Training

We don’t just train youth to climb — we train them to build a future. Our program includes real business education so youth can turn their skills into opportunities.

Youth learn how to:

  • Understand what homeowners and clients look for
  • Communicate clearly and respectfully with clients
  • Show up on time, prepared, and ready to work
  • Stay organized with tools, schedules, and responsibilities
  • Build a reputation based on reliability and respect
🌿 Community Projects

🌿 Community Projects

Training is tied directly to service. Youth don’t just practice skills — they use them to restore and uplift the community.

  • Cleanup days in neglected areas
  • Tree trimming and hazard removal
  • Light building and repair projects
  • Beautification and restoration efforts
