Youth Training Programs

Program Overview

Our youth training program is built around real work, real responsibility, and real support.

  • Tree climbing fundamentals and safety
  • Tool handling and site awareness
  • Environmental stewardship and care
  • Teamwork, leadership, and communication
Tree Climbing & Safety

Youth learn from experienced mentors how to:

  • Use harnesses, ropes, and helmets correctly
  • Assess tree health and structural risk
  • Move safely in elevated environments
  • Respect the environment while working in it
Community Projects

Training is tied directly to service:

  • Cleanup days in neglected areas
  • Tree trimming and hazard removal
  • Light building and repair projects
  • Beautification and restoration efforts
