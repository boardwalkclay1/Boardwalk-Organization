🌱 Why Your Donation Matters

Every donation directly supports youth training, mentor time, safety gear, transportation, and community restoration projects. You’re not just giving money — you’re giving skills, safety, and opportunity.

Boardwalk Clay is built on real work, real mentorship, and real community impact. Your support fuels everything we do.

🌳 What Your Donation Supports

Youth Training

  • Helmets, gloves, ropes, harnesses
  • Training days with professional mentors
  • Transportation to service sites
  • Meals on long cleanup days

Mentor Support

  • Mentor time and supervision
  • Safety oversight and training materials
  • Fuel and equipment maintenance

Community Restoration

  • Cleanup supplies
  • Dump fees
  • Tools and hazard removal
  • Beautification and restoration materials

🍂 How Your Donation Works

We use a single PayPal QR code and donation button. After you donate, our system organizes your donation on our end.

Here’s what happens:

  1. You donate using PayPal (QR or button)
  2. PayPal returns you to our success page
  3. Our system logs your donation
  4. We categorize it (youth, mentor, community, etc.)
  5. You receive a confirmation

This keeps everything clean, secure, and organized — without needing multiple PayPal accounts.
