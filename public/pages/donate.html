💚 General Donation

Your donation fuels everything we do — from youth training to community restoration. Every dollar helps us reach more neighborhoods, train more youth, and support more mentors.

Your contribution helps fund:

  • Cleanup supplies and restoration materials
  • Youth training gear and safety equipment
  • Mentor support and certification costs
  • Fuel, tools, ropes, gloves, helmets, and more

🌲 Support Mentors

Mentors are the heart of our mission. They train youth, lead projects, ensure safety, and guide young people toward leadership and responsibility.

Your support helps provide:

  • Professional safety gear and climbing equipment
  • Transportation to service sites
  • Time spent training and supervising youth
  • Ongoing certifications and skill development

🌱 Support Youth Training

Youth in our program learn real skills — tree climbing, safety, teamwork, environmental care, and even business fundamentals. Your support helps them grow into confident, capable leaders.

Your donation helps cover:

  • Helmets, harnesses, gloves, and ropes
  • Training program enrollment
  • Transportation and meals on service days
  • Tools and equipment for hands-on learning

🧰 Equipment Donations

We gladly accept equipment donations that help youth and mentors work safely and effectively in the field.

Useful items include:

  • Ropes, saddles, harnesses, helmets
  • Gloves, boots, safety glasses
  • Hand tools, saws, rakes, shovels
  • Climbing gear and protective equipment
