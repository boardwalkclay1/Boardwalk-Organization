🌳 How We Help the Community

Boardwalk Clay Organization focuses on the places most people overlook — the overgrown lots, unsafe trees, trash‑filled corners, and forgotten spaces that drag down entire neighborhoods.

Our work includes:

  • Neighborhood cleanup days that restore pride and safety
  • Tree work and hazard removal led by trained youth and mentors
  • Light building and repair projects for community spaces
  • Youth‑led service missions that build leadership and confidence
  • Environmental education woven into every project

Every cleanup, every tree trimmed, every restored space is a step toward a stronger community.

🤝 How You Can Help the Community

You can step into the story with us. Whether you have time, tools, or a location that needs care — you can make a real impact.

  • Volunteer at cleanup and tree work days
  • Offer tools, trucks, or equipment
  • Host a youth training or community event
  • Connect us with neighborhoods that need help
  • Share our mission with local leaders and neighbors

💚 How You Can Help Us Help the Community

Your support multiplies our impact. When you sponsor or donate, you’re fueling real work in real neighborhoods.

  • Sponsor mentors so they can train and lead youth safely
  • Sponsor youth so cost is never a barrier to training
  • Donate to cover gear, safety equipment, fuel, and materials

🌱 Sponsorships Overview

We offer two primary sponsorship channels that directly support our mission:

  • Sponsor a Mentor – support the leaders who train and protect youth
  • Sponsor a Youth – fund training, gear, and safety for a young person

📅 Community Events & Service Days

We host recurring and special events focused on hands‑on service and community restoration.

  • Neighborhood cleanup days
  • Tree trimming and hazard removal
  • Youth‑led service projects
  • Skill‑building workshops in the field
