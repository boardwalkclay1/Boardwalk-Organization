Community Service & Impact

How We Help the Community

Boardwalk Clay Nonprofit focuses on neglected areas—overgrown lots, unsafe trees, trash‑filled corners, and forgotten spaces.

  • Neighborhood cleanup days
  • Tree work and hazard removal
  • Light building and repair projects
  • Youth‑led service projects with mentor guidance
  • Environmental education woven into every action
How You Can Help the Community

You can step into the story with us:

  • Volunteer at cleanup and tree work days
  • Offer tools, trucks, or locations that need care
  • Host a youth training or community event
  • Share our work with neighbors and local leaders
How You Can Help Us Help the Community

Your support multiplies our impact:

  • Sponsor mentors so they can train and lead youth in the field.
  • Sponsor youth so cost is never a barrier to training.
  • Donate to cover gear, safety equipment, fuel, and materials.
Sponsorships Overview

We offer two primary sponsorship channels:

  • Sponsor a Mentor – support the leaders who train and protect youth.
  • Sponsor a Youth – fund training, gear, and safety for a young person.
Community Events & Service Days

We host recurring and special events focused on hands‑on service:

  • Neighborhood cleanup days
  • Tree trimming and hazard removal
  • Youth‑led service projects
  • Skill‑building workshops in the field
